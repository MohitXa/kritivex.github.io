"Why are we still learning the old way?"

I often visited schools and spoke to students in Classes 5-10. I realized something worrying: while the world was moving towards AI and Robotics, these bright young minds lacked guidance and practical knowledge about modern technology.

That was the moment Kritivex was born.

We are not just a software company; we are on a mission to solve this problem. By partnering with schools and building easy-to-use educational apps, we ensure that every student gets the right guidance at the right time.
